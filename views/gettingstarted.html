Welcome to Weekly Planner!

Weekly Planner is a simple and easy to use planner app that lets you plan out your week, and also have a list of your goals and accomplishments. You can create a list of goals in the Goals tab, and then you can head over to your Dashboard to plan out what you need to do to reach those goals, and then once you've accomplished them, you can view your whole record under the Accomplishments tab.

Thanks for choosing Weekly Planner, and we hope you'll get lots of use out of it!
